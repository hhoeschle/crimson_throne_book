Red Mantis attack in Vencarlo's house by MrVergee

Watch
Artisan Crafts / Miniatures / Fantasy 2015-2016 MrVergee
#assassin #freebooter #pathfinder #reaper #warhammer #paizo #redmantis #curseofthecrimsonthrone #orisini #psomminiature #dwarvenforge #vencarlo #rescuefromoldkorvosa (show more)
The companions are looking for fencing master Vencarlo Orisini, but he is not at home. Instead two Red Mantis Assassins are waiting for the party. The miniatures are by Reaper, Warhammer and Freebooter, the scenery is form Dwarven Forge, Psomminiature and a homebrew hearth. Our adventures are described in the campaign journals section of Paizo's message boards under Mister Vergee's Curse of the Crimson Throne.
